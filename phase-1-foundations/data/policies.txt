AutoElite Motors - Dealership Policies
========================================

WARRANTY POLICY
---------------
New Vehicles:
All new vehicles sold at AutoElite Motors come with the full manufacturer's
warranty. Coverage varies by brand but typically includes:
- Bumper-to-bumper: 3 years or 36,000 miles (whichever comes first)
- Powertrain: 5 years or 60,000 miles
- Corrosion/rust-through: 5 years or unlimited miles
- Roadside assistance: 3 years or 36,000 miles
Hyundai and Kia vehicles include 5-year/60,000-mile basic and 10-year/100,000-mile
powertrain warranties.

Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles:
All CPO vehicles undergo a rigorous multi-point inspection and come with:
- Extended powertrain warranty: up to 7 years or 100,000 miles from original
  in-service date
- 12-month/12,000-mile comprehensive limited warranty
- Complimentary CARFAX vehicle history report
- 24/7 roadside assistance for the duration of the warranty

RETURN POLICY
-------------
AutoElite Motors offers a 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee on all vehicle purchases:
- Return the vehicle within 7 calendar days of purchase for a full refund.
- Vehicle must have fewer than 500 miles added since purchase.
- Vehicle must be returned in the same condition as delivered, free of damage.
- Aftermarket modifications void the return policy.
- Refunds are processed within 10 business days to the original payment method.
- Trade-in vehicles that were part of the transaction will be returned if still
  in inventory, or fair market value will be provided if already sold.

FINANCING OPTIONS
-----------------
AutoElite Motors partners with over 20 national and local lenders to offer
competitive financing for every credit profile.
- New vehicle APR: Starting at 2.9% for 36 months (qualified buyers)
- Used vehicle APR: Starting at 4.5% for 36 months (qualified buyers)
- Loan terms available: 24, 36, 48, 60, and 72 months
- No prepayment penalties on any loan
- Requirements: Valid driver's license, proof of income, proof of residence,
  and minimum down payment of $500 or 10% of purchase price (whichever is less)
- First-time buyer programs available with co-signer option
- Credit union and outside financing welcome

TRADE-IN POLICY
---------------
We accept trade-ins of all makes and models in any condition.
- Trade-in values are based on current fair market value using Kelley Blue Book,
  NADA guides, and live auction data.
- Appraisals are valid for 7 days from date of evaluation.
- Outstanding loan balances will be paid off directly; any positive equity is
  applied to the new purchase or refunded.
- Negative equity may be rolled into the new loan, subject to lender approval.
- Bring your vehicle title, registration, and all keys/remotes to the appraisal.

TEST DRIVE POLICY
-----------------
We encourage customers to test drive before purchasing.
- Valid driver's license and proof of insurance required.
- Test drives are limited to 30 minutes and a pre-approved route unless otherwise
  arranged with a sales manager.
- Customers must be 21 years or older (or 18+ with a parent/guardian present).
- A sales associate will accompany all test drives.
- Schedule a test drive online at www.autoelitemotors.com or call (555) 123-4567.

SERVICE & MAINTENANCE
---------------------
Our factory-trained service technicians are here to keep your vehicle in top shape.
- Service hours: Monday-Friday 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM
- Complimentary multi-point inspection with every service visit
- Genuine OEM parts used on all repairs
- Free shuttle service within a 15-mile radius
- Loaner vehicles available for major repairs (by reservation)
- Online service scheduling at www.autoelitemotors.com/service
- Oil change and tire rotation packages starting at $49.95
- All service work comes with a 12-month/12,000-mile warranty on parts and labor
