# Welcome to AutoElite Motors Assistant

This is an AI-powered assistant for AutoElite Motors car dealership.

## Modes

- **Customer Mode**: Ask about our inventory, financing options, promotions, and policies.
- **Employee Mode**: Access internal information including complaints, training materials, and procedures.

## Getting Started

Simply type your question below to get started. Use the settings panel to switch between Customer and Employee mode.

## Example Questions

**Customer**: "What SUVs do you have under $40,000?"
**Customer**: "Tell me about your financing options"
**Employee**: "Show me recent customer complaints"
**Employee**: "What is the return policy procedure?"
